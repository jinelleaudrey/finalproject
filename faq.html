PAIR ME

1. How does pair me! work?

The pair me! match test is a series of questions especially designed to help match you with a pet that will fit your lifestyle. Once you submit your answers, our system will be able to match you up with up to five pets that would be a good fit for you.

2. Why isn't there a question about picking an age for the pet?

Desired age for the pet is not an option because we believe all pets should be adopted regardless of age, sex, or breed.

3. Do I have to take the Pet Pair Match Test?

No, you do not have to take the match test. It would be helpful for yourself to see what kind of pet would be better for your lifestyle, it is not however mandatory.

ADOPTING A PET

1. Will the adoption happen through pet pair?

The adoption proccess does not happen through Pet Pair. Each adoption facility has its own protocols and system for adoptions. For more information please refer to the website of the facility.

2. In order to adopt a pet do I have to take the Pet Pair Match Test?

You do not have to take the match test in order to adopt a pet, but it is recommended.

3. How long does the adoption process take?

The amount of time the adoption process takes depends from where you are adopting the pet. Each facility may have different guidelines. It can take anywhere from an hour if you go directly to the adoption center, to a couple months depending on which facility your dersired pet is from.

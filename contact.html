about us

The creators of this world-changing Website are Alissa, Jinelle, Sydrah, and Valentina. As part of the final project for Girls Who Code summer immersion program at Facebook campus, we wanted to express our love for pets:) We wanted to help people near Redwood City to become pet owners and get more dogs and cats to have amazing owners. Unlike other pet stores and adoption sites, we focused on the owner's interests and personality rather than age or breed of the pet.

If you have any questions, us at petpair@gmail.com.
